The story behind the name

Shanky was always one to buck the trend - he liked the idea of Irish Whiskey - but he did not like the burn - he liked the taste of Cream - but he did not like the texture... and he loved Stout - which is of course, black! So he had a lightbulb moment... and Shanky's Whip was born.
